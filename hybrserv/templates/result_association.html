$def with (result, form) HybrServ v0.12

HybrServ v0.12

$form.experiment
$if form.sequence:
Sequence $form.sequence
Rate $result['rate'] /M /s
C.I. 95% $result['rLow'] - $result['rHigh']
Simulation time $result['myTime'] s
nForward, nReverse   $result['nFor'] - $result['nRev']
Substrate $form.substrate
Temperature $form['temperature'] C
$else: Please supply a sequence.

Hybridization: Computation ends when 30 successful trajectories are found, or after 30 seconds, whichever occurs earlier. The maximum sequence length is 100 nt. Dissociation: Computation time is unrestricted, but maximum sequence length is 20 nt. Threeway branch migration: Computation time is unrestricted, but the sum of sequence lengths should be less than 50 nt. Dissociation and branch migration rate computation is based on a rare event method, hybridization is based on First Step Mode.

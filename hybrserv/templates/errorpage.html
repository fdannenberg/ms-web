Hybridization rate computation is based on First Step Mode. The computation ends when N successful trajectories are found or after max(N,30) seconds, whichever occurs earlier. The maximum sequence length is 100 nt. Dissociation and toehold-mediated branch migration rate computation is based on an unpublished rare event simulation method.
